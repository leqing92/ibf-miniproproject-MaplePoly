Game Rule

Jail

If a player steps on "Jail" two times or step on "Go to Jail", then the player will be sent to jail for two turns.


Properties

  1. A player who lands on any unowned property may buy it from the bank at the listed purchase price.
  2. The palyer can directly build an house/houses/hotel after purchase the land.
  3. Player to pay rent to the owner of "Utility" based on the dice they rolled when they landed on the property.
  4. "Utility" and "Railway" rent increase as the player owns more of them.
  5. A player can sell their property back to bank but bank only will pay for the land value (without house).

Bankruptcy

A player is immeadiately declared as bankrupt if they have insufficient cash to pay rent/tax even though they have property can be sold.

The bankrupt player's properties will be released back to market.


Win

The last player survive in the game will be the winner.
